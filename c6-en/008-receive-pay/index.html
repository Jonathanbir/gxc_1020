    • International 008 Toll Free Service

  • The domestic customer with fix/mobile number of Chunghwa can apply this service
  • The caller from abroad can dial the Int'l 008 number to talk to the domestic customer free of charge.
  • The Domestic customer with IDD fixed-term contract can enjoy discount on international call. Otherwise, we provide automatically the major customer discount.

    • Application Method

    The domestic customer applies for the I-008 number and provide it to the foreign caller who wish to to talk to the domestic customer free of charge, and the domestic customer pays for their international calls.
