  • Provides POR and IOR ocean region satellite communications services.

  • Provides uplink and reception services for the customers.

    • Introduction to Product

    Occasional Use TV – OUTV

    Product Name:

    Occasional Use TV – OUTV


    Product Description:

    TV stations or TV program content providers deliver their News or Sports programs to the ChungHwa Telecom's Yangmingshan or Fangshan Earth Station.

    Chunghwa Telecom's Earth Station provides C or Ku band antenna systems and RF equipment for customers services.

    Satellite uplink equipment and satellite transponder bandwidth are provided by Chunghwa Telecom. to uplink and broadcast the TV programs.

    Or satellite downlink equipment and satellite transponder bandwidth are provided by Chunghwa Telecom to receive the TV programs.

    Transmission or reception by Chunghwa Telecom's uplink or downlink equipment for the broadcast of news or sports programs to domestic and foreign media.

    Provides satellite communication services at Pacific Ocean Region(POR) and Indian Ocean Region(IOR).

    Rate Calculation:

    (1).Fixed-period lease: lease period of half-year, one year, two years, three years…and etc.

    (2).Temporary lease: at least 30 minutes needed or one hour, two hours…and etc.. After booking confirmed, it cannot be cancelled.


    Functions and Characteristics:

    (1).Uses satellite transponder,ground segement of satellite and terrestrial circuits to provide radio and television program relay.

    (2).Planning and design services related to system operations for the customer.

    (3).Provides satellite uplink services.

    (4).Provides overall services of TV-relay.

    (5).Provides contribution TV-relay services.

    (6).The "Yangmingshan Earth Station" in the north and the "Fangshan Earth Station" in the south can provide 24h/7d network management services and instantly monitor the quality of communications.

    The Introduction for C Band Transponders of ST-2 Satellite:

    120 W traveling-wave tube amplifier

    Coverage: Asia, India, Middle East, North Africa, Northern Australia, Western Pacific, Indian Ocean


    The Introduction for Ku Band Transponders of ST-2 Satellite:

    150 W traveling-wave tube amplifier

    Coverage: Northeast Asia, Southeast Asia, Indochina, Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines
